Questions? We've Got Answers.

NO-SHOW SOCKS VERSUS CREW SOCKS: WHICH IS BETTER?

The best sock length depends on your preference and the shoes you're wearing. No-show socks are ideal for a clean look with sneakers and loafers, while crew socks offer more coverage and can be paired with various footwear.

WHAT IS THE MOST SUSTAINABLE MATERIAL FOR SOCKS?

Sustainable materials like organic cotton, TENCEL™ Lyocell (derived from eucalyptus), and recycled fibers are excellent choices for eco-conscious socks due to their lower environmental impact.

WHAT ARE SUSTAINABLE SOCKS?

Sustainable socks are made using environmentally friendly materials and production processes that minimize waste, conserve resources, and reduce carbon footprint. Look for certifications and brands that prioritize ethical and sustainable practices.
